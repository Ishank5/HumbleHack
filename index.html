In this hackathon, your challenge is to build an Android app that creatively combines data from at least two different public APIs to make something useful, fun, or innovative.

Your goal is to take real data from the web, merge it in a smart way, and turn it into a functional app with a clean, user-friendly UI.

üí° Problem Statement

Build an Android app that uses at least two different APIs and combines their data in a creative way.

Your app should meaningfully integrate and display data fetched from both APIs, showing your understanding of API calls, data parsing, and user interface design.

You are free to choose any APIs that are publicly available and free to use.
