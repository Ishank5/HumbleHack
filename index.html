üß© Overview

You've just learned how to use APIs in Android apps, now it's time to put that skill to the test!

In this hackathon, your challenge is to build an Android app that creatively combines data from at least two different public APIs to make something useful, fun, or innovative.

Your goal is to take real data from the web, merge it in a smart way, and turn it into a functional app with a clean, user-friendly UI.

üí° Problem Statement

Build an Android app that uses at least two different APIs and combines their data in a creative way.

Your app should meaningfully integrate and display data fetched from both APIs, showing your understanding of API calls, data parsing, and user interface design.

You are free to choose any APIs that are publicly available and free to use.

üìç Judging Rounds

Your journey through HumbleHack will consist of multiple evaluation checkpoints designed to assess your progress and innovation

Checkpoint 1

Mystery Analysis Round

Kickstart your hackathon journey with a twist of mystery! In this round, the real challenge lies in the unknown, participants won‚Äôt be told the evaluation criteria until the round begins. Stay alert, think smart, and be ready for anything! This round is designed to test your adaptability, teamwork, and presence of mind as you tackle the unexpected. Get set to embrace the mystery and prove your versatility!

Checkpoint 2

Mentor's Round

Our expert judges and mentors will personally visit each team to dive deep into your project idea. This is your opportunity to present your concept, discuss your API integration strategy, and receive valuable feedback. Mentors will assess the creativity of your approach, the feasibility of your implementation, and the potential impact of your solution. Make your pitch count!

Checkpoint 3

Elimination Round

The competition intensifies! Teams will be evaluated on their technical implementation, code quality, API integration, and overall development progress. Judges will assess how effectively you've combined multiple APIs, the robustness of your error handling, and the polish of your user interface. Only the top 5-6 teams demonstrating exceptional innovation and technical excellence will advance to the final round.

Final Round

Grand Pitching & Winner Announcement

The moment of truth! Finalists will present their complete applications to the judging panel and audience in a live demonstration. Showcase your app's functionality, explain your creative API combinations, and highlight what makes your solution unique. After all presentations conclude, winners will be announced in a closing ceremony celebrating the innovation and hard work of all participants. Good luck!
